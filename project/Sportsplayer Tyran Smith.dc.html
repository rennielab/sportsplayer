Founder · CEO · Player Agent

Tyran
Smith.

More than a sports agent — a visionary and a role model whose leadership inspires a new generation of athletes, and those who work behind the scenes.

With a rich history in professional Rugby League and an uncompromising dedication to integrity, Tyran Smith has pioneered a unique approach to sports management — one that puts the athlete's character and humanity at the forefront.

His career began on the playing field, where he gained invaluable insights into the inner workings of professional sport. His experience as a professional player instilled a deep understanding of the challenges and pressures athletes face. Recognising that a shift in sports culture was needed, he ventured into management — determined to make a positive change.

Sportsplayer Management was founded on Tyran's two cardinal values: honesty and integrity. His contributions reach beyond contracts and negotiations, into the very heart of what it means to be an athlete and a human being.

"A shift in sports culture was needed. So we built one — human first, athlete second."
