We represent the human, then the athlete.

At Sportsplayer, we passionately represent the best interests of our clients — tirelessly aiding them in reaching their full potential in the ever-evolving arena of professional sport.

We operate under one motto: Human First, Athlete Second. Because there's a substantial difference between merely making it and truly thriving as a dominant, preeminent professional.

At the heart of our operations are two fundamental values — honesty and integrity. They form the backbone of our entire approach, and they're why we believe players should be rewarded commensurately with their worth.
